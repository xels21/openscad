# Summary

Horizontal filament spool holder for 50mm, up to 1kg, spool.
A sealed deep groove skateboard ball bearing, type 608, fits in the 22mm hole of part 1. 
Part 3 connects the bearing 8mm hole and part2.
Can be printed on a small 3D printer like my Fabrikator Mini.

Part3 version 2: 
Wider footage for stabilisation and bigger shaft diameter to fit tight in the 8mm bearing hole (needs some sand paper finishing). No more hot glue needed.


# Print Settings

Printer: Fabrikator mini
Rafts: No
Supports: Yes
Resolution: 0.2mm
Infill: 50%
